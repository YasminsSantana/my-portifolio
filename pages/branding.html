Branding Projects

Pata Fofa

A complete branding identity project for "Pata Fofa", a cozy and friendly local pet shop. The project included logo design, color palette selection, typography, and promotional materials, all aimed at conveying warmth and care for pets.

Iteach Rebranding

This project involved a full rebranding for "Iteach", an online educational platform. The goal was to modernize its image, making it more appealing to a younger audience while maintaining a professional and trustworthy feel. The new identity was applied across their website and mobile app.

Dona Moranga

"Dona Moranga" is a brand of artisanal pumpkin jams and sweets. The branding and packaging design focused on a rustic, homemade aesthetic, using organic elements and warm colors to evoke a sense of tradition and delicious, natural flavor.
